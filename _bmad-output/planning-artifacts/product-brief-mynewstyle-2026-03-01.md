# Product Brief: mynewstyle

## Executive Summary

mynewstyle is an AI-powered visagism platform that delivers hyper-personalized hairstyle and grooming consultations at scale — without requiring human stylists or visagism professionals. Users upload a selfie, complete a lifestyle and preference questionnaire, and receive a comprehensive consultation that includes face shape analysis, personalized haircut recommendations, grooming tips, and AI-generated visual previews showing them with the recommended styles. The platform serves both male and female audiences through gender-specific style paths, making professional-grade image consulting accessible to everyone.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Most people struggle to find the hairstyle that truly suits their face shape, features, and lifestyle. Professional visagism — the science of harmonizing appearance with facial geometry — is typically available only through expensive in-person consultants, making it inaccessible to the vast majority of people. As a result, most individuals rely on guesswork, fleeting trends, or barber/salon suggestions that may not account for their unique facial proportions and personal style.

### Problem Impact

- **Financial barrier**: Professional visagism consultations cost significantly more than a standard haircut, pricing out most consumers
- **Knowledge gap**: Barbers and stylists are skilled at execution but rarely trained in formal visagism analysis
- **Decision anxiety**: People often leave salons dissatisfied because they couldn't visualize the result beforehand
- **Missed potential**: Without proper guidance, people settle for styles that don't complement their natural features, affecting confidence and self-image
- **Scale limitation**: Even where visagism expertise exists, it cannot scale — each consultation requires a trained professional's time

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

- **Generic online quizzes**: "What's your face shape?" tools provide oversimplified, one-size-fits-all recommendations without accounting for hair texture, lifestyle, or individual proportions
- **Social media/trends**: Instagram and TikTok showcase styles on models and influencers, but what looks great on one face shape may not suit another
- **Barber/salon consultations**: Dependent on individual stylist knowledge, inconsistent, and limited to the professional's subjective opinion
- **Existing apps**: The few visagism apps that exist (e.g., the Lovable-based visagist bot) are limited to male audiences, lack visual simulation of results, and offer narrow analysis without lifestyle context
- **Professional visagists**: Deliver excellent results but are expensive, scarce, and inherently unscalable

### Proposed Solution

mynewstyle is a platform that automates the entire visagism consultation process through AI agents:

1. **Gender-specific onboarding**: Users select male or female style paths, unlocking tailored analysis frameworks (e.g., beard recommendations for men, hair length/layering for women)
2. **Photo capture and AI facial analysis**: The system analyzes the user's face shape (oval, square, round, triangular, oblong, heart, diamond), facial proportions, and visible hair characteristics
3. **Lifestyle questionnaire**: Captures style preferences, daily routine, professional context, and grooming habits to personalize recommendations beyond just face geometry
4. **Comprehensive AI consultation**: Delivers 2-3 recommended hairstyles with detailed justifications rooted in visagism principles, plus grooming and styling tips
5. **Visual simulation**: AI generates realistic preview images showing the user with each recommended style — enabling confident decision-making before visiting a barber or salon
6. **Consultation history**: Users can revisit past consultations, favorite results, and try new photos over time

### Key Differentiators

- **Hyper-personalization at scale**: AI agents deliver the quality of a professional visagism consultation without human bottlenecks, enabling thousands of simultaneous consultations
- **Visual "see yourself" previews**: Unlike any generic recommendation tool, users see AI-generated images of themselves with recommended styles — the most powerful confidence-builder before committing to a cut
- **Dual-gender platform**: Serves both male and female audiences with gender-specific analysis paths, unlike competitors that focus solely on one demographic
- **Holistic analysis**: Combines facial geometry with lifestyle context (occupation, daily routine, personal style) for recommendations that work in the user's real life, not just in theory
- **Accessible pricing**: By replacing human consultants with AI, the platform can offer premium-quality visagism at a fraction of the cost
- **No expertise required on the business side**: The operator doesn't need to hire visagism professionals — AI agents handle the entire consultation pipeline

## Target Users

### Primary Users

#### Persona 1: Lucas — The Style-Conscious Young Professional (Male)

**Profile:** Lucas, 27, works as a marketing analyst at a mid-size company. He cares about his appearance and knows that looking sharp matters for his career and social life, but he's not a fashion expert. He gets a haircut every 3-4 weeks at a local barbershop.

**Problem Experience:** Lucas scrolls Instagram for haircut inspiration but never knows if a style will actually work for his face. He's had multiple disappointing experiences — asking his barber for a fade he saw online, only to realize it doesn't suit his round face shape. He doesn't know what "visagism" is, but he knows something is off when certain cuts don't look right on him.

**Workarounds:** He screenshots celebrity haircuts and shows them to his barber, hoping for the best. Sometimes he asks friends for opinions, but gets vague feedback.

**Success Vision:** Lucas uploads a selfie, gets told he has a round face shape and why certain cuts create better visual balance, sees 2-3 recommended styles with AI previews showing *him* with those cuts, and walks into his barbershop with a clear, informed request. He leaves feeling like a million bucks.

#### Persona 2: Camila — The Reinvention Seeker (Female)

**Profile:** Camila, 32, is a freelance graphic designer going through a life transition — new city, new chapter. She wants a hairstyle that reflects who she's becoming, not who she was. She has wavy hair and an oval face but doesn't know how to leverage her natural features.

**Problem Experience:** Camila has spent hours on Pinterest boards but feels overwhelmed by options. She's tried two different salons in her new city with mixed results. Each stylist suggested something different, and neither considered her lifestyle (she works from home, values low-maintenance styles, but wants to look polished for client meetings).

**Workarounds:** Pinterest boards, salon consultations (inconsistent), asking friends via group chats with selfies.

**Success Vision:** Camila uploads her photo, answers questions about her lifestyle and preferences, and receives recommendations that balance her desire for reinvention with practical daily styling needs. The AI preview lets her *see* the transformation before committing, giving her confidence to go to a new salon with a clear direction.

#### Persona 3: Rafael — The Low-Effort Guy (Male)

**Profile:** Rafael, 35, is a software engineer who keeps things simple. He's worn the same basic cut for years because he doesn't know what else to try and doesn't want to spend time researching. His wife has suggested he try something new, and he's open to it — but he needs the process to be effortless.

**Problem Experience:** Rafael finds the whole hairstyle selection process intimidating and confusing. He doesn't follow style trends and wouldn't know where to start. Going to a barbershop and saying "whatever you think is best" has produced mediocre results.

**Workarounds:** Tells the barber "same as last time" or "keep it short." Avoids the decision entirely.

**Success Vision:** Rafael takes one selfie, answers a few quick questions, and gets a clear recommendation with visual proof that it suits him. The entire process takes under 5 minutes. He shows the AI preview to his barber and gets exactly what was recommended.

### Secondary Users

#### Platform Administrator / Business Operator

The person running the mynewstyle business. They don't need visagism expertise — the AI handles that. They need a dashboard to track consultations, monitor user growth, view engagement metrics, and manage the platform. Their success is measured by consultation volume, user satisfaction, and conversion rates.

#### Barbers & Stylists (Indirect Users)

While not platform users themselves, barbers and stylists benefit when clients arrive with clear, well-informed requests backed by AI analysis. Clients showing AI previews gives stylists a precise reference to execute, reducing miscommunication and improving satisfaction for both parties.

### User Journey

#### Discovery Phase
- **Trigger**: User sees an ad on social media (Instagram/TikTok), gets a recommendation from a friend, or searches "what haircut suits my face shape"
- **First impression**: Landing page with bold before/after visuals and a clear 3-step promise: Photo → Analysis → Your New Look
- **Hook**: The "see yourself with a new style" visual simulation is the primary draw

#### Onboarding Phase
- **Gender selection**: User picks Male or Female path — the UI, language, and recommendations adapt accordingly
- **Photo upload**: Simple selfie capture or gallery upload with guidance on lighting and angle
- **Questionnaire**: 5-8 questions about lifestyle, style preferences, daily routine, and grooming habits (kept fast and engaging — under 2 minutes)

#### Core Experience
- **AI processing**: Animated loading experience showing the analysis in progress (face mapping visualization, style matching animation)
- **Results delivery**: Face shape identified and explained, 2-3 recommended hairstyles with visagism-backed justifications, grooming/styling tips
- **"Aha!" moment**: User taps "See How I Look" and sees an AI-generated preview of *themselves* with the recommended style — this is the emotional peak

#### Post-Consultation
- **Action**: User saves their favorite result, optionally shares it, and takes the recommendation to their barber/salon
- **Return**: User comes back to try a new photo, explore alternative styles, or check their consultation history
- **Long-term value**: The platform becomes their go-to resource before any haircut appointment — a personal style advisor always in their pocket

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**1. Gender Selection Gateway**
- Binary male/female selection screen at the start of the flow
- UI theme, language, and recommendation framework adapt to selection
- Clean, bold visual design with gender-appropriate styling cues

**2. Photo Upload & Capture**
- Camera selfie capture with on-screen guidance (lighting, angle, framing)
- Gallery upload option as alternative
- Frontend image compression/resizing before submission (≤ 800px width to optimize API payload)
- Basic photo validation (face detected, adequate quality)

**3. Lifestyle Questionnaire**
- 5-8 questions tailored by gender selection:
  - Style preference (classic, modern, bold, minimal)
  - Daily routine / maintenance level willingness
  - Professional context (corporate, creative, casual)
  - Hair type / current hair situation
  - Grooming preferences (beard style for men, hair length comfort for women)
- Engaging, fast UI — goal: under 2 minutes to complete

**4. AI Facial Analysis & Consultation Engine**
- Face shape detection (oval, square, round, triangular, oblong, heart, diamond)
- Facial proportion analysis
- Hair type assessment from photo
- Generation of comprehensive written consultation:
  - Face shape identification with explanation
  - 2-3 recommended hairstyles with visagism-backed justifications
  - Beard/grooming suggestions (male path) or layering/length suggestions (female path)
  - Styling tips and product recommendations

**5. AI Visual Preview ("See How I Look")**
- AI image generation that takes the user's actual photo and applies the recommended hairstyle
- Realistic output preserving the user's face, skin tone, and features
- One preview per recommended style (2-3 total)
- Before/after comparison view (side-by-side or slider)

**6. Results Page**
- Comprehensive results display with clear visual hierarchy:
  - Face shape badge and analysis
  - Numbered recommendation cards with justifications
  - "See How I Look" button per recommendation
  - Styling tips section (parsed into structured, scannable format)
  - Beard/grooming section (male) or hair care section (female)
- Creative loading animation during AI processing (user's photo with transformation effects)

**7. User Authentication & Profile**
- Email/password and Google OAuth registration/login
- User profile with consultation history
- Ability to favorite/save results
- Ability to start a new consultation with a new photo

**8. Landing Page**
- Hero section with clear value proposition and before/after visuals
- "How it works" section (3 steps: Photo → Analysis → Your New Look)
- CTA to start consultation
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Bold, urban visual identity (dark theme, vibrant accent colors)

### MVP Success Criteria

**Launch Gate (Go/No-Go for public launch):**
- AI face shape detection accuracy ≥ 85% (validated against manual assessment on 100+ test photos)
- AI visual preview quality rated ≥ 3.5/5.0 by beta testers for realism
- End-to-end consultation flow completes in ≤ 5 minutes
- Error/timeout rate < 5% across consultation flow
- Both male and female paths tested with ≥ 50 users each

**30-Day Post-Launch Validation:**
- ≥ 500 completed consultations
- Consultation completion rate ≥ 80% (of users who upload a photo)
- "See How I Look" usage ≥ 60% of consultation recipients
- User satisfaction ≥ 3.8/5.0
- Organic referral rate ≥ 5%

**Scale Decision Point (90 days):**
- Sustained growth in weekly consultations (week-over-week)
- Retention: ≥ 20% of users return for a second consultation
- Positive qualitative feedback on recommendation relevance
- AI cost per consultation trending toward ≤ $0.50
